We are a research lab in the Institute of Cognitive Science (ICS) at the University of Colorado Boulder, located on the East Campus in the Center for Innovation and Creativity (CINC).

    Directions:
  1. From Denver, take US-36 W to Boulder
  2. Take the exit onto Foothills Parkway just before entering Boulder
  3. Drive north on Foothills Parkway
  4. Pass Baseline and Colorado Boulevard, at the traffic light turn left onto Arapahoe
  5. Drive west on Arapahoe, at the first traffic intersection turn right onto 38th Street
  6. Turn left onto Exposition Drive
  7. CINC is a large 2 story building on the left side
  8. The main entrance is on the southwest side
  9. Enter through the doors and keep left
  10. Our lab is in the Western portion of the building
