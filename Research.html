Aphasia treatment and clinical practice

TheCenter for Aphasia Research and Treatmentat theShirley Ryan AbilityLabin Chicago is using our systems and virtual agents to help people with aphasia and other acquired neurologic communication disorders get the long-term intensive care they need, whether at the clinic or home. Findsoftwareto treat aphasia on their web site.

Computational treatment using virtual agents can be effective. Forexample, in an NIH-funded randomized controlled cross-overstudyof eight participants receiving a speech and language treatment for chronic aphasia, following a stroke, training with more visual cues compared to fewer visual cues resulted in a large effect size and two times faster learning.
